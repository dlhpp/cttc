Matt Winkler

Matthew Winkler, USATT Certified National level Coach, Regional Umpire, and Referee has been the Phoenix Club Coach since the beginning of 2008. His highest rating was 2311. Matthew is the Number 1 player in Arizona.

Matthew currently attends USATT coaching clinics every year keeping up on the latest strategies and strokes used around the world.

Matthew currently coaches more than 30 players from Monday through Saturday. He coaches all ages from 6-90 years of age, as well as all levels, from beginners to advanced. Also, Matthew has held table tennis classes in schools.

If you want to schedule lessons with him at the Phoenix Table Tennis Club or at his Central Phoenix Facility, E-Mail him at matt@tabletennisandmore.com or phone him at 602-678-1234.
