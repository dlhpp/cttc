About CTTC

The CTTC was born on May 14, 2017 through the efforts of Shawn Hsu, Hong Yang, Mike Fuschino and Sam Huang.

Shawn dreamed up the idea of crusading for tables at the local recreation centers. Hong Yang suggested contacting our Chandler City Councilman, Sam Huang, to see if Sam could pull some strings. Sam backed the idea and Shawn and Mike presented the case before the Chandler City Council We now have 4 tables and a room at the Chandler Community Center.

Sam Huang suggested we start an offical club in order to have a voice with the city so that we can get more tables and more playing time. And that's the spark that led to the creation of the Chandler Table Tennis Club.
