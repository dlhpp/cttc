Welcome to the ping pong club in Chandler Arizona!

The Chandler Table Tennis Club (CTTC) is a non-profit association organized in May 2017.

Our volunteers work closely with the City of Chandler to promote table tennis in the East Valley community. 

We believe table tennis is a sport for all people: young or old, tall or short, male or female.

We welcome beginners as well as experienced players to play with us.
