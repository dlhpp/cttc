Ladder Tournament

On Sunday April 29 2018, the Chandler Table Tennis Club started a ladder tournament which was suggested and organized by Frank Salamat. Rules and details will be posted later.

We had 15 participants in the first round. We dedicated one table to the ladder tournament. Players were free to play as normal on the other tables and were called over when it was their turn to compete in the ladder so it was efficient and fun.
