Welcome to the ping pong playground at the Chandler Community Center!

Chandler Table Tennis Club (CTTC) is a non-profit association organized in May 2017.

Our volunteers work closely with the City of Chandler to promote table tennis in East Valley community. 

We believe table tennis is a sport for all people, men or women, tall or short, young or old. We welcome beginners as well as experienced players to play with us.
